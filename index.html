Rental Program

Dress for the Win — Without the Investment

Not every show calls for a full wardrobe purchase. Our rental program puts premium show attire within reach for every rider, every class.

01

Browse & Select

Browse available rental pieces by size, style, and color. Find your perfect look for upcoming shows — jackets, shirts, breeches, and accessories all available.

02

Reserve Your Dates

Reserve your rental for show weekend. We'll confirm availability and coordinate pickup or delivery to the showground, depending on the event location.

03

Ride & Return

Compete in style, then simply return the piece after the show. We handle all cleaning. Damage waiver options available for added peace of mind.

How It Works

Simple. Transparent. Fair.

01
Submit Your Inquiry

Tell us what you have — photos, brand, size, condition. We'll review and confirm what we can list.

02
Drop Off or Ship

Bring items to us at a local show or Utah location, or ship to our address. We'll inspect and photograph everything.

03
We List & Sell

Your items go live in our Square shop and at shows. We handle all buyer inquiries, payment, and shipping.

04

Once sold, you receive your agreed-upon percentage. Payout via Venmo, PayPal, or check — your choice.

Our Story

Born in the Barn. Built for the Ring.

Main Ring Bling was born out of a simple truth every Arabian horse person knows: the main ring is where everything comes together. The years of training, the bond with your horse, the early mornings and late nights — it all culminates in those few minutes under the lights.

We believe your attire should match that moment. That's why every piece in our collection — whether purchased, rented, or consigned — is chosen with the eye of a competitor and the heart of a horse person.

Based in Utah and deeply rooted in the Arabian horse community, Main Ring Bling serves riders across Region 7 and beyond — from valley shows to Scottsdale. We're not just a shop; we're your ringside partner.
